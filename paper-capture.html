ScanOnce

There's been a common theme in the document imaging world for decades. That is, if you aren't happy with the way an image looks the first time, you change your scanner settings and try again. Rinse, repeat. It's a vicious cycle that leads to forgotten settings and a lack of consistency across scan stations or periods of time. The problem can be especially bad when running a visually diverse set of documents through a single cleanup profile.

Connect to over 400 document scanners using ISIS or TWAIN drivers, and centrally manage image cleanup settings to bring consistency and quality to your end-product. Grooper also features integrations for standalone devices like IBML's imageTrac and the ColorTrac SmartLF wide format scanners.

Rapid Image Review

For organizations capturing large volumes of paper documents, image quality can be a large source of frustration. Our Image Review interface allows you to Groop images together by height, width, intensity, and aspect ratio. This naturally brings images with similar defects together and allows you to rapidly correct issues without having to look at every single image.

After we made the switch to Grooper, our time to review image quality and perform all necessary rescans went from about an hour per banker's box down to less than 5 minutes. When you have a paper processing facility the size of ours, this equates to well over $100K each year in savings.

Avis Bray, Director of the 4D Center at BIS
