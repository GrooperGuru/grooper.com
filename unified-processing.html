5-Phase Batch Processing Approach

We realized even in the earliest stages of Grooper's life the importance of parallel processing. Conditioning documents for complete data collection requires a tremendous amount of computational cycles for image processing, text collection, and data analysis. This was the correct architecture to maintain reasonable processing times without having to compromise our ability to get every piece of data we wanted.
